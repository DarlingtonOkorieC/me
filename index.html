About Me

I am fascinated with community development, web development, writing and storytelling, and, of course, film.

I am a web developer with a proficiency in php laravel as well as in Wordpress CMS. As a web developer, I have had [and certainly still do] the previlege of working for several organizations, of which include: Vision Alive Foundation, Pan African Spark Hub, Ebenezer Chibundu Consults, CreTechnologies, Innovation Growth Hub, Ace Awards, Aba, The Free School Foundation, Konetapp, Vision Incubation Hub, among others as technical person and/or web developer.

I am an addicted storyteller, finding expression through written and spoken literature. This includes writing novels and screenplays, as well as in spoken word renditions. I have written for Rimrock Hotels, Ace Awards Aba, Startup Grind, Aba, Startup Weekend, Aba, Google Developer Group(GDG), and currently serve as project coordinator for He's A Hero's Network where I have the role of creating several well curated content as part of my responsiblity for the organization.
I am currently working on a Lagos Sci-fi novel as well as a christmas thriller for the year.
Moreso, I do voice-overs and acting performances for various enteratinment outfits.

I am about community development and impact. I have volunteered for several programs. I am a tutor for Reap In Technology, a web designer for the Free School Foundation, a writer for Startup Grind, and a developer working to ensure that the annual International Youth Day Conference organized by Vision Alive Foundation remain successful. For the past five years, I have been a mentor at Technovation, Nigeria and at BrainFacio, an organization poised to equip young students and girls with technical skills for the 21st century.
